Privacy Policy

Pomodoro Focus Timer · Last updated: 22 May 2026

The short version: Pomodoro Focus Timer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline. Everything you record stays on your device.

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy covers the mobile application Pomodoro Focus Timer ("the app") for iOS and Android. By using the app you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Information we collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. The app has no account system, no login, and no registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The app records your focus sessions (date, session type, duration, and completion time) purely so it can show you statistics and streaks. This information is stored only on your device — in a local database on Android (Room) and in local app storage on iOS (Core Data). It is never uploaded anywhere and is never accessible to us or any third party.

3. How your data is used

Session records stored on your device are used solely to:

  • Display your timer history, statistics, and the focus heat map;
  • Calculate your current and best streaks;
  • Remember your timer preferences between launches.

You can permanently erase all recorded data at any time using the "Reset all statistics" option in the app's Settings. Uninstalling the app also removes all of its data.

4. Internet access

The app does not require or use an internet connection. It makes no network requests and contains no analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services.

5. Notifications

With your permission, the app sends local notifications to alert you when a focus session or break ends. These notifications are generated on your device and scheduled with the operating system. They are never sent through a server and contain no personal data. You can disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings or in your device's system settings.

6. Permissions

  • Notifications — used only to alert you when a timer finishes. Granting this permission is optional; the app remains fully usable without it.

The app requests no other sensitive permissions. It does not access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or location.

7. Third-party services

Pomodoro Focus Timer integrates no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics providers. No data is shared with any third party because no data ever leaves your device.

8. Children's privacy

The app is safe for all ages. Because it collects no personal information, it is compliant with children's privacy regulations such as COPPA and the GDPR-K.

9. Data security

Because your data never leaves your device, it is protected by your device's own security (passcode, biometric lock, and storage encryption). We hold no servers and no copies of your information.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new "Last updated" date. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@appfactory.example.
