Privacy Policy

QR & Barcode Scanner+  ·  Last updated: 22 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the QR & Barcode Scanner+ mobile application (the “App”) handles information. We have designed the App so that it does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information whatsoever.

The short version: The App has no servers, no accounts, no advertising, and no analytics. Everything happens on your device. Nothing about you or your scans ever leaves your phone.

Information We Collect

None. The App does not collect personal information, device identifiers, location data, contacts, or usage analytics. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not require any sign-in.

Camera Access

The App requests permission to use your device’s camera. The camera is used solely to view a live image so that QR codes and barcodes within it can be detected in real time.

  • The camera feed is processed entirely on your device.
  • No photos, video, or frames from the camera are saved by the App.
  • No images or camera data are transmitted off your device.
  • No image is analyzed for any purpose beyond detecting a code.

Photo Library Access

The App can optionally scan a code from an existing image you choose from your photo library. When you select an image, it is decoded on your device to look for a QR code or barcode. The selected image is not copied, stored, or transmitted by the App, and access is limited to the single image you pick.

Scan History

The App keeps a history of your most recent scans (up to 200 entries) so you can retrieve them later. This history is stored only on your device — in the App’s private local storage — and is never uploaded anywhere. You can delete individual entries or clear the entire history at any time from within the App. Removing the App deletes this history permanently.

On-Device Barcode Engine

The Android version of the App uses Google’s ML Kit barcode scanning engine. The barcode model is bundled inside the App and runs fully offline on your device; it does not send images or scan results to Google or any other server. The iOS version uses Apple’s built-in AVFoundation framework, which also runs entirely on-device.

No Advertising and No Tracking

The App contains no advertising, no ad SDKs, no third-party trackers, and no analytics frameworks. We do not build a profile of you and we do not use tracking technologies of any kind.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services that receive your data. When you choose to open a scanned link, place a call, send an email, or share content, your device’s own apps handle that action under their respective privacy policies — the App itself transmits nothing.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal information for us to lose or expose. Your scan history remains within the protected, app-private storage of your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new “Last updated” date. Material changes will be reflected in an App update.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to support@appfactory.example.
