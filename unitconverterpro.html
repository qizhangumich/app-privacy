Privacy Policy

Unit Converter Pro · Last updated: May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Unit Converter Pro (the “App”) handles your information. The short version: the App collects nothing about you.

1. Information We Collect

None. Unit Converter Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. We have no servers, no analytics, and no user accounts.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves your favorite conversion pairs and your conversion history (up to the last 100 conversions) locally on your device using your operating system’s standard preference storage. This data:

  • never leaves your device;
  • is not accessible to us or any third party;
  • can be cleared at any time from within the App;
  • is removed entirely when you uninstall the App.

3. Internet Access

The App works completely offline. It makes no network requests of any kind. All conversion factors are bundled inside the App.

4. Permissions

The App requests no device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive resource.

5. Third-Party Services

The App contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, and no third-party tracking technologies of any kind.

6. Children’s Privacy

Because the App collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published with a new “Last updated” date. Since the App collects no data, any future changes will be minor and informational.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@appfactory.example.
