Privacy Policy

Tip Calculator Deluxe · Last updated: 22 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tip Calculator Deluxe ("the app") handles your information. The short version: the app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information whatsoever.

Information We Collect

None. Tip Calculator Deluxe does not collect any personal data. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information. The app does not require an account and you can use every feature without signing in.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves your most recent calculations (up to 20) so you can review them later. This calculation history is stored only on your device using the operating system's standard local storage (UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android). This information:

  • never leaves your device;
  • is not transmitted to us or to any third party;
  • is not accessible to us in any way;
  • can be cleared at any time using the "Clear" option in the app's history screen;
  • is removed completely when you uninstall the app.

Tracking and Analytics

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking technologies, and no third-party SDKs. We do not use cookies, device identifiers, or any form of usage tracking.

Third-Party Services

Tip Calculator Deluxe does not integrate with any third-party services. No data is sent to external servers because the app works entirely offline.

Permissions

The app requests no special permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or files.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is ever updated, the revised version will be published with a new "Last updated" date. Since the app collects no data, we do not expect material changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the app's listing on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
